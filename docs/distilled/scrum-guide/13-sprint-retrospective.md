# Sprint Retrospective

**TL;DR:** Plan ways to **increase quality and effectiveness**.
Final event of the Sprint. Timebox **3 h** for a one-month Sprint.

## Purpose

To plan ways to **increase quality and effectiveness**.

## Inspection scope

- Individuals
- Interactions
- Processes
- Tools
- The **Definition of Done**

Inspected items vary with the domain.

## What the team does

- Discuss what went well, what problems occurred, and how those
  problems were (or were not) solved.
- **Identify assumptions** that led the team astray; **explore
  their origins**.
- **Identify the most helpful changes** to improve effectiveness.
- **Address the most impactful improvements as soon as possible** —
  may even be added to the **Sprint Backlog for the next Sprint**.

## Position and timebox

- **Last event of the Sprint.**
- **Up to 3 hours** for a one-month Sprint; shorter for shorter Sprints.

## When this applies

- Every Sprint
- After incidents or quality regressions
- When the team feels stuck — designed for surfacing it

## Anti-patterns

- Skipped because "nothing to improve" or "we're too busy"
- Ritual-only retrospective (same template, same vague items)
- Improvements identified but never acted on
- Improvements **never** make it into a Sprint Backlog
- Manager attends and people stop being open
- Treating the retrospective as a **complaints session** without
  generating concrete actions
