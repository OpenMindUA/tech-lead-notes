# NUPP principles — distilled

**NUPP** = *Nearly Universal Principles of Projects*. Open
non-proprietary resource (<https://nupp.guide>) — usable as a lens to
re-interpret any other PM system. Six principles, intentionally
generic and short.

| # | Principle | One-liner |
|---|-----------|-----------|
| 1 | [Results over affiliations](01-results-over-affiliations.md) | Don't be loyal to a school — use whatever method actually works. |
| 2 | [Preserve energy and resources](02-preserve-energy.md) | Resources, including your decision-making energy, are finite — apply 80/20. |
| 3 | [Always be proactive](03-always-be-proactive.md) | Reactive default is biological; project work needs proactive action (e.g. risk management). |
| 4 | [Weakest link](04-weakest-link.md) | All domains interact; over-investing in one (e.g. scheduling) wastes the rest. |
| 5 | [Clear purpose](05-clear-purpose.md) | Never adopt a practice without a tangible purpose; otherwise it's cargo-cult. |
| 6 | [Repeatable elements](06-repeatable-elements.md) | Ad-hoc work is expensive; build checklists and cycles to free up cognition. |
