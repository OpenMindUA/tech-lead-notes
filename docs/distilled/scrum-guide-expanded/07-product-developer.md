# Product Developer

**TL;DR:** SGE-equivalent of SG2020 "Developers". May be **human or
automated**. **At least one** Product Developer **must be human**.
Accountabilities are extended.

## Definition

> "Product Developer is a role and an accountability. All Product
> Developers together should possess all the skills needed to
> create Increments. The combined skill set is often referred to as
> cross-functional."

## Properties (delta from SG2020 Developers)

- May be **human or automated** (AI agents). At least **one human**
  PD required.
- Multiple human PDs improve **cognitive diversity** for complex
  work.
- **Single-Product focus** — best results when focused solely on
  one Product.
- If PO or SM works on Sprint Backlog items, they do that work **as
  Product Developers**.
- Roles a PD may take: collaborator, creator, **champion of
  technical quality**, discovery, delivery, **value validation**.
- **Step-down rule:** a PD who is neither willing, ready, nor able
  to be a professional should step down.

## Accountabilities (always, collectively)

| # | Accountability | Notes |
|---|----------------|-------|
| 1 | Create an emergent plan in the Sprint Backlog for the Sprint Goal | Same as SG2020 |
| 2 | Instill quality by adhering to and **improving** the **Definition of Output Done** | DoD renamed; PDs improve it (SGE addition) |
| 3 | Create **at least one usable Increment every Sprint** | New explicit accountability |
| 4 | **Learning, often through data guided by Definition of Outcome Done** | New |
| 5 | Adapt the plan each day toward the Sprint Goal | Same |
| 6 | Hold each other accountable as professionals | Same |
| 7 | **Net improvement** | New explicit accountability |

## When this applies

- Defining a "Developer" role on cross-functional teams (research,
  data, design, engineering)
- Onboarding AI agents as PDs (with human-in-the-loop guardrails)
- Performance-management aligned to SGE accountabilities
- Quality review and continuous improvement of the DoD itself

## Anti-patterns

- "Developer = programmer only" framing on the team
- All PDs are AI — **no human PD violates SGE rule**
- PDs sized work but didn't **improve** the DoD over time
- PDs ignore **value validation** — only deliver outputs
- PDs not held to **professionalism** standard
