# Scrum Guide Expansion Pack (v2026.1) — distilled

Cards distilled from the *Scrum Guide Expansion Pack* (v2026.1) by
Ralph Jocham, John Coleman and Jeff Sutherland — an explicit
**adaptation** of the 2020 Scrum Guide that adds theory, expands
roles, and refines artifacts and commitments.

> **License:** CC BY-SA 4.0. These cards are derivative; same license
> applies. The Expansion Pack is **not** the canonical Scrum Guide —
> for the immutable framework see the [2020 Scrum Guide](../scrum-guide/index.md).

> **How to read these cards.** SGE assumes you already know Scrum.
> These distilled cards focus on **what's new or different** from the
> 2020 Scrum Guide. For unchanged elements, follow the cross-ref to
> the corresponding [Scrum Guide 2020 card](../scrum-guide/index.md).
