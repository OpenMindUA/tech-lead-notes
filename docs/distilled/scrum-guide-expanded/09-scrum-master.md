# Scrum Master — SGE extensions

**TL;DR:** SGE recasts the Scrum Master as a **change agent at all
organizational levels** who is **accountable for the effectiveness
of the Scrum Team, Stakeholders, Supporters and affected people**.
Must be human. Anti-pattern list is unusually explicit.

## What stays from SG2020

- True leader who **serves**.
- Causes removal of impediments.
- Ensures Scrum events take place.
- Coaches self-management and cross-functionality.
- Single person, not a committee.

## What SGE adds

### Identity (sharpened)
- **Must be human.** Cannot be AI.
- **Change agent** working at all org levels and across business
  areas.
- **Leads by example.**
- Understands **complexity** and is skillful at "enabling the next
  right thing."

### Behaviors **expected**
Guide, coach, mentor, teacher, observer, impediment remover, agent
of change, effectiveness facilitator, continuous improvement champion.

### Behaviors **prohibited (anti-patterns explicit)**
> "The Scrum Master is **neither** a team administrator, status
> manager, taskmaster, rule-dictator, meeting-room booker, report
> dashboard administrator, chairperson, hero, coordinator, **nor an
> in-absentia Scrum Master leaving everything to 'self-management'**."

### Effectiveness scope (broadened)
Accountable for the effectiveness of:
- Scrum Team
- Stakeholders
- Supporters
- "Affected people"

### Service categories (SGE expands SG2020's 3 to ~5)

#### Serves the Scrum Team, PO, and Supporters
Helping everyone understand Scrum theory; enabling continuous
improvement; fostering timely intentional interactions; ensuring
suitable **Definition of Output Done**; ensuring events happen;
removing impediments; coaching toward self-management;
foster evidence-informed confidence; foster change agency.

#### Serves the Scrum Team
Formation, upskilling, continuous improvement; clear concise PBIs
that deliver value; vigilant about purposeful collaboration and
**Definition of Output Done** adherence and **Definition of Outcome
Done** focus.

#### Serves the Product Owner
Effective **Product Goal** definition + Backlog management;
**emergent Product planning** for complex work; **express outcomes
as measures via the Definition of Outcome Done**; clear concise
PBIs; **focus on value realization**.

#### Serves the Stakeholders (NEW)
Help adopt empirical approach for complex work; foster
"Stop putting items in progress; **start finishing items**"
mantra; facilitate Stakeholder collaboration; help focus on **value
realization**.

#### Works with the Supporters (NEW)
Lead, train, coach Supporters in adoption; clarify what's blocking
adoption; facilitate **disciplined emergent change**; foster
organizational change toward **ease of delivery vs ease of
management**.

#### Works with the organization
Lead/train/coach the org; plan and advise adoptions; work with
related departments; remove barriers.

### Tenure and posture
- Calling where helping others succeed is reward enough.
- Doesn't seek the spotlight; gives credit to others; takes
  responsibility for failures.
- Longer tenure helps the team toward potential — **only if** the
  PDs collectively develop self-management.
- **Parent-style behavior does not foster self-management.**
- **Step-down rule:** a SM who is neither willing, ready, nor able
  to be an agent of change should step down.

## When this applies

- Defining SM job description and performance criteria in SGE org
- Diagnosing SM dysfunction (use the explicit anti-pattern list)
- Multi-team / multi-organization adoption coaching
- AI tooling integration (SM ensures empiricism is preserved)

## Anti-patterns (per SGE, verbatim direction)

- Team administrator
- Status manager
- Taskmaster
- Rule dictator
- Meeting-room booker
- Report-dashboard administrator
- Chairperson
- Hero
- Coordinator
- **In-absentia** "self-management" excuse for absence
- Parent-style coaching that prevents self-management
