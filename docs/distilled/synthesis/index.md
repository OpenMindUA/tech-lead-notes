# Synthesis cards — cross-source

These cards **combine material from multiple sources** in this
repository. Where individual per-source cards answer
*"what does framework X say about Y?"*, synthesis cards answer
*"what does the whole corpus say about Y, and how do the
frameworks differ?"*

> **License:** because these cards mix material from CC-BY (UtS)
> and CC-BY-SA sources (Scrum Guide, SGE, DORA), the synthesis
> tier is **CC-BY-SA-4.0** (the more restrictive ShareAlike
> license prevails). See [About the distilled tier](../about.md).

## How to use

- **Start in [agent-index.md](../../agent-index.md)** for the per-source view.
- **Drop into a synthesis card** when the question spans multiple
  frameworks ("how does Scrum's DoD relate to PMBOK 7's quality
  principle?").
- **Follow the cross-refs** at the end of each synthesis card to the
  corresponding per-source distilled card or full Tier-3 chunk.
