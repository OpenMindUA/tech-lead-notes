# Performance domains — distilled

Eight domains PMBOK 7 uses to **enrich** any chosen methodology.
They are sets of concerns to verify your methodology covers
adequately for *your* project. If your method is thin in a domain,
extend it (don't replace it).

| # | Domain | One-liner |
|---|--------|-----------|
| 1 | [Stakeholders](01-stakeholders.md) | Identify → Understand → Prioritize → Engage → Monitor — continuously. |
| 2 | [Team](02-team.md) | Centralized vs distributed PM, sponsor role, leadership development. |
| 3 | [Development approach & life cycle](03-development-approach.md) | Predictive vs adaptive — driven by product nature, not preference. |
| 4 | [Planning](04-planning.md) | Planning is continual; cover all knowledge areas; meta-plans tailor coverage. |
| 5 | [Project Work](05-project-work.md) | Two-way dance between plans and reality; capture follow-up items. |
| 6 | [Measurement](06-measurement.md) | Forecast targets to completion; pick metrics aligned with real goals. |
| 7 | [Delivery](07-delivery.md) | Requirements → Deliverables → Activities; limit WIP; verify before delivery. |
| 8 | [Uncertainty](08-uncertainty.md) | Risk register / follow-up register; Monte Carlo only when justified. |
