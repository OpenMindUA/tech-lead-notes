### Manage by exception

We must have proper delegation in projects, to use the decision making power of more people, create buy-in, and save energy. To do so, we set tolerances for each level of management, and if the consequences of a decision are below the threshold, we let them decide without interfering, but if they’re above that, we expect them to escalate it to the higher level.
