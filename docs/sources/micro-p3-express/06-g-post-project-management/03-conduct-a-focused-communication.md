# G3 - Conduct a focused communication

  

If there are stakeholders other than the hats involved in the post-project management cycle, we need to inform them of the results.

Project Manager Hat

  

Who should be informed of the results of the benefits evaluation?

When the organization is larger than the project team, everyone in that organization should be informed of the results of the evaluation within the limits of confidentiality. This is a reminder for everyone of why we do the projects, which is helpful for on-going and future projects.
